Baked Chicken Breast

Description

Tasty, succulent protein. An easy recipe that yields a juicy muscle building staple!/p>

Ingredients
  • boneless chicken breast
  • kosher sea salt
  • black pepper
  • olive oil
  • selected spices (paprika, oregano, basil, etc)
Steps
  1. Set the oven to bake 450 degrees. Prepare the chicken on a baking sheet or glass oven ware
  2. Smother chicken in olive oil, salt and pepper chicken, sprinkle choice spices onto chicken
  3. bake for 15-25 minutes depending on thickness of cut. Check at the 15 minute mark if inside is still pink.
  4. Take chicken out of oven to cool. Serve with your choice of carbs (rice, potatoes, use for sandwich) and vegetables
