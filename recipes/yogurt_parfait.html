Yogurt Parfait

Description

A healthy dessert consisting of fresh fruit and yogurt. Perfect for breakfast or anytime really. High in protein

Ingredients
  • 2% Greek Yogurt
  • your choice of fruit (berries, bananas, apples, mangos etc)
  • crunchy granola
Steps
  1. Add a scoop of yogurt into a cup or mason jar
  2. Layer fruit, fresh or frozen on top of the yogurt
  3. Repeat step 1 & 2
  4. Finish with some crunchy granola
