Salmon Sushi

Description

A delectable favourite from the east, features rice and raw fish wrapped in seaweed

Ingredients
  • rice
  • salmon
  • seaweed
  • rice vinegar
  • water
  • soy sauce
  • avacado (optional)
Steps
  1. Place the seaweed on a bamboo mat, then cover the sheet of seaweed with an even layer of prepared sushi rice. Smooth gently with the rice paddle.
  2. Layer salmon, avacado (optional) on the rice, and roll it up tightly. Slice with a sharp knife and enjoy with soy sauce.
